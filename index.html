About

How did we get here?

  • 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    In July 2017, two undergraduate friends wanted to work on a space-focused project with the intention to compete in the Spaceport America Cup. No such experience was available in Australia, so we created our own opportunity.

  • January 2018

    Project Takes Form

    By the new year, UQ Space had grown to twenty members across all engineering disciplines, with over 27,000 words on paper and a clear goal to work towards.

  • March 2018

    The Australian Space race Begins

    The Australian Youth Aerospace Association announced the 'Australian Universities Rocket Competition' (AURC).

  • May 2019

    The Australian Universities Rocket Competition

    The team looks to assess their progress in development & learning by competing in the AURC with a goal of reaching 10,000ft.

  • Stretch Goal

    The Spaceport America Cup

    UQ Space looks to compete abroad at the Spaceport America Cup in the future.
